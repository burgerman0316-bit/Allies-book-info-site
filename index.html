ALLIES

BY: ALAN GRATZ

Summary

View Summary

D-Day: June 6, 1944. A day that would change the course of history. Alan Gratz follows the perspectives of various young people caught in the chaos of the invasion.

D-Day Timeline

06:00 AM

The invasion begins. Allied forces approach the beaches of Normandy.

06:30 AM

H-Hour: First wave of troops land on Omaha and Utah beaches.

12:00 PM

Fighting continues inland as paratroopers secure key bridges.

Midnight

The end of the first day; the beachheads are established.

Characters

Dee Carpenter

An American soldier with a secret: he's actually a German immigrant.

Samira

A young French girl working with the Resistance to find her mother.

James

A Canadian paratrooper jumping into the dark to secure the inland front.

Bill

A tank driver pushing forward through the chaos of the beaches.

Monique

A girl on the beach who dreams of being a nurse amidst the battle.

Henry

A Black American medic saving lives under heavy fire.
