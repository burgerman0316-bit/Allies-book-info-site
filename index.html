Theme

The Meaning of "Allies"

The main theme is Cooperation. Alan Gratz shows that D-Day could only succeed if people of different races, genders, and countries acted as "Allies." Another theme is Identity, specifically seen through Dee, who struggles with being a German-born soldier fighting for America.

Characters

DEE

A 16-year-old US soldier from Nebraska. His secret is that he was born in Germany and his real name is Dietrich.

SAM

An African American medic on Omaha Beach. He deals with Jim Crow-era racism while risking his life to save others.

JAMES

A British paratrooper who jumps into France before H-Hour to destroy Nazi defenses.

MONIQUE

A French girl living near the coast who ends up helping the Resistance and Allied wounded.

DOROTHY

A female journalist (inspired by true history) who sneaks onto the beach to report the truth.

BILL

A tank driver who has to survive the chaotic landing on the shore.

Glossary

Higgins Boat: The flat-bottomed boat that landed troops on the beach.


D-Day: June 6, 1944—the day the Allied forces invaded Nazi-occupied France.


French Resistance: French citizens who fought the Nazis using secret missions.
